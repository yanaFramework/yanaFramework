To ease your search this program automatically searches for possibly related words as well.

For example, when searching for "new" you will also get sites which contain similar phrases like "news". But you won't get soundalikes like "newbies", that you were probably not searching for.

Please note, that this feature was intended for German texts and is less effective on English websites. Even though it also slightly improves the quality of search results for most other languages.
